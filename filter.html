Manuel Schneider
ÄNGSTLI
18.4.2026 18 - 22
Auf dem Wolf 11, Basel
ÄNGSTLI is an exhibition of new paintings by Manuel Schneider. The exhibition is situated in an emptied out studio and will only be open to the public for one Saturday evening. The german Angst translates to fear in english. The suffix -li is common in swiss german dialect to render any noun to its diminuitive, its belittlement.
